Nutrition

Nutrition is the biochemical and physiological process by which a person uses food to support its life. It provides us with nutrients, which can be metabolized to create energy and chemical structures. Failure to obtain the required amount of nutrients causes malnutrition. Make sure to eat a balanced diet with plenty of fruits, Meat, vegetables, and whole grains.

Exercise

Exercise is physical activity that enhances or maintains fitness and overall health. It is performed for weight loss or maintenance, to aid growth and improve health. Regular physical activity helps you maintain a healthy weight and reduces your risk of chronic diseases.

Common Health Issues

Overweight may increase your risk for many health problems especially if you carry extra fat around your waist. Stay informed about common health such as :

    1. Type 2 diabetes
    2. High blood pressure
    3. Heart disease
    4. Stroke
    5. Kidney disease
    6. Cancers
